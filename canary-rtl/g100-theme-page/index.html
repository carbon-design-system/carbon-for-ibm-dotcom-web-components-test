About the 2x Grid It’s fundamental to everything we design. The 2x Grid is the geometric foundation of all the visual elements of IBM Design, from typography to columns, boxes, icons, and illustrations. It provides structure and guidance for all creative decision-making. The basic unit of 2x Grid geometry is the 8-pixel square mini unit. Multiples of mini units compose the dimensions of columns, rows, boxes, along with their margins and padding. The mini unit adapts to your content while maintaining a consistent visual rhythm. Carousel controls The Icon buttons and pagination to the bottom right provide navigational controls for browsing the carousel. They are required as part of this component. Number of cards The recommended amount of content is a minimum of 5 cards and maximum of 15 cards to avoid excessive clicking for the user. Related content Make sure content in the carousel is related. No essential content Do not add essential content to a carousel. Essential content could include navigational or wayfinding content links. Most important content Prioritize the most important content at the beginning of the carousel.
